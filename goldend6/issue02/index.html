The Golden D6 Issue #02

The much anticipated second issue of The Golden D6. Six months after Issue One seems like more than enough time to wait for the second issue and I am disgustingly proud and excited for you to finally get a look at it.

In this issue

  • Shizune Paints shows us how to paint blue armour
  • Allied Wargaming present Bolt Action for the Hobbyist
  • Evil Nevets painting show off an awesome looking Combined Army Fraacta from Infinity
  • We’re introduced to the cast and crew at Metalhead Minis
  • Haaaaave you met … Andy?
  • A wonderfully narrative battle report from Chris for Warmahordes
  • Joe Baird shows you how to make custom decals for an Imperial Knight
  • The Lost and the Very Damned make dungeon tile scenery
  • We feature the Seraphon army of the massively talented Amy Snuggs
  • Plus some other cool things too …
